💡 Tips
────────────────────────────
• Keep images equirectangular (2:1 aspect ratio)
• Use EXR for lighting realism, HDR for quick 360 backgrounds
• For web / Three.js: load HDR via RGBELoader and PMREMGenerator
• For VR: sphereGeometry(500, 60, 40), invert scale.x = -1
• Always align the brightest spot to your sun light direction
